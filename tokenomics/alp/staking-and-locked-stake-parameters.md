# Staking and Locked Stake Parameters for ALP 🔐

## Duration Locking

Locking your ALP for a set duration earns additional USDC revenue and ADX rewards, rewarding longer-term aligned collateral depositors for contributing to the stability of Adrena's user experience.

## Early Unlock

Duration Locked ALP can be prematurely unlocked by forfeiting between **15–40%** of the principal (the exact amount depends on the duration of the remaining lock). This forfeited ALP is automatically burned.

## Liquid Staking

Staked $ALP that isn't Duration Locked can be claimed at any time without a waiting period.

# Mutagen (Points System) 🧪

Mutagen is a dynamic and elusive resource that powers your progress on Adrena. It's designed to reward not just performance, but overall trading engagement. Unlike the pre-season that relied on raw PnL, starting from Season 1, Mutagen was introduced as the core ranking metric. This decision was made to reward all types of traders more fairly — although rest assured that volume and PnL stays kings.

## How Mutagen is Earned

Mutagen is distributed and scored off-chain, based on on-chain activity. Key sources include:

- Trading volume
- PnL performance
- Seasonal mechanisms such as quests, streaks, and other limited-time events
- Partnerships and special events

## History

Mutagen was revealed on February 1st. While leaderboard ranking started from this date, Mutagen was back-generated from any earlier trading activity. Note that this leaderboard only counts toward the $ADX airdrop, not the season rankings.
